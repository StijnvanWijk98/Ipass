Update position.

This function updates the position of the particle.
It does two checks, collision with other particles and out of bounce. You are obligated to give delta_time (double), particles (array), id(int).
When the particle collides with another particle, the position doesn't change.
If collided, speed is {0.0,0.0}.
When the ball is out of bounce on one of the axis, the position is set to the exact min or max.
The speed is also set to 0 on the corresponding axis.
Call example: part.updatePosition(0.25, arr, 2);
